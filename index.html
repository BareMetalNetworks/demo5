French Roast Colombian

Our French Roast Colombian Coffee is full bodied with rich caramelized sugars, low acidity and with a robust bittersweet finish. We roast to the second crack, allowing natural oils to excrete, creating a shiny dark bean with a light oily surface. Developing a rich bold pleasing flavor of a dark roasted coffee without an overly bitter or charred tastes.

Brazilian Roast

Our Brazilian Full City Roast Coffee is a mellow, pleasant coffee and low in acidity. Full City Roast develops nice bittersweet cocoa notes ending with a clean finish. Brazilian coffees are not particularly complex, but solid crowd pleasing coffees with excellent body, appealing to those who prefer a less acidic cup.

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

As the birthplace of coffee, Yirgacheffe produces beans that are often considered the gold standard for all coffees. The exotic tastes of the harvests from the highlands of Ethiopia are complex, intense, and preferred by many coffee connoisseurs.
